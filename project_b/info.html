POST MALONE

Austin Richard Post (born July 4, 1995), known professionally as Post Malone, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, record producer, and musician. Malone has gained acclaim for blending a range of genres including pop, hip hop, trap, pop-rap, rap rock, and cloud rap. Malone is known for his introspective songwriting and variegated vocal styles on his music. He first attained recognition in 2015 following the release of his debut single "White Iverson".
